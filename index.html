About Us

The Weekly Playlist was curated by Luke Titus in order to spread some of his favorite music to the world in the form of vinyl, CD and MP3. Luke digests a large amount of music regularly and felt that he was able to offer a diverse and interesting selection of music to the world of audiophiles with a refined palette.

Every week, The Weekly Playlist offers a new selection of 6 different projects for purchase. We are an official vendor of music and pay royalties to all artists. We have gone through all of the steps necessary to provide the best music for you while being able to support these amazing artists!

Were you busy last week and as a result were unable to check out the amazing selection of music that we had available for you? No problem! Check the list below to find out the music that you missed!

Brand New - Daisy
